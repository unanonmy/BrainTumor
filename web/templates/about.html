Brain Tumor Detection and Classification

Motivation

The main motivation behind Brain tumor detection is to not only detect tumor but it can also classify types of tumor. So it can be useful in cases such as we have to sure the tumor is positive or negative, it can detect tumor from image and return the result tumor is positive or not. This project deals with such a system, which uses computer, based procedures to detect tumor blocks and classify the type of tumor using Generative Adversarial Networks and Convolution Neural Network Algorithm for MRI images of different patients.

About Brain Tumor

A cancerous or non-cancerous mass or growth of abnormal cells in the brain is known as brain tumor . These tumors can start in the brain or elsewhere in the body and can spread to the brain . Symptoms of brain tumour includes increasing headache , blurred vision , loss of balance,confusion and seizures. There is always need of advancement when it comes to medical imaging. Early detection is a way to control the brain tumor. There are many cases that are handled by the early detection and decrease the death rate.

There are different types of brain tumor and they are

  1. Glioma
  2. Meningioma
  3. Pituitary Tumor

Brain Tumor can be both benign and malignant.Among the different tumor types ,Meningioma is Benign and Glioma is Malignant and Pituitary tumor is very rarely found.

Glioma is a type of tumor that occurs in the brain and spinal cord. Gliomas begin in the gluey supportive cells (glial cells) that surround nerve cells and help them function.

A meningioma is a primary central nervous system (CNS) tumor. This means it begins in the brain or spinal cord. Overall, meningiomas are the most common type of primary brain tumor.

A pituitary tumor is a tumor that forms in the pituitary gland near the brain that can cause changes in hormone levels in the body. This illustration shows a smaller tumor (microadenoma). Pituitary tumors are abnormal growths that develop in your pituitary gland.

Problem Statement

Brain Tumor detection and classification is vital as a health indicator. This model takes MRI images as input and classifies them into different brain tumor classes.MRI is mainly used to get images of the human body and cancerous tissues because of its high resolution and better quality and less radiation. Through this model,the patient will be able to know the stage of brain tumor the patient is in .

Proposed Work

Various techniques have been developed by researchers to detect brain tumor. Main methods used for developing this model are CNN and GAN

GAN is used for unsupervised learning and is used to copy and capture variations in dataset. GAN can be used to generate data when there is no enough data and it mainly consists of Generator and Discriminator.

CNN is mainly used for analysing images,data analysis and data classification and detecting patterns. CNN mainly consists of Convolutional Layer, Max Pooling Layer, Fully Connected Layer .
